Growth Market Dynamics

Lacey represents one of the fastest-growing submarkets in the Puget Sound region, with population growth exceeding 15% over the past decade. The area benefits from its proximity to Joint Base Lewis-McChord, one of the largest military installations in the United States, providing a stable employment base of over 40,000 personnel.

The local economy is diversified across government, healthcare, retail, and technology sectors, creating resilient demand for retail services. With median household incomes 20% above the national average and strong demographic trends, this investment offers exceptional potential for long-term value appreciation.
